
## What Prompts Worked Well
- What are the office hours?
- How do I contact IT?
- Where is the company portal?
- Office opening time
- IT support number

These prompts worked well because they matched the trigger phrases configured in the topics.


## Accurate and Inaccurate Responses

### Accurate Responses
- The agent correctly answered questions about office hours.
- The agent successfully provided IT support contact information.
- The agent correctly shared the company portal link.


### Inaccurate Responses
- The agent could not answer questions outside the configured topics.
